Christines Curtains

Small business based in Kidderminster.

Curtains

At Christine’s Curtains, I personally create each set of curtains with care and attention to detail. Whether you need simple drapes or something more decorative, I offer a range of fabrics and styles to suit your home or office. Each curtain is crafted to enhance your space with quality and affordability in mind.

Blinds

Discover practical elegance with my handmade blinds. From basic roller blinds to custom-designed options, I provide solutions that fit your windows perfectly. Using durable materials and straightforward designs, I ensure your blinds offer both privacy and functionality without compromising on style.

Cushions

Add comfort and charm to your décor with my handmade cushions. Made from quality fabrics and tailored to your preferences, each cushion is crafted to bring a personal touch to your living space. Whether you need simple accents or statement pieces, I create cushions that reflect your style and make your home cozy.

About Me

With over 25 years of experience, I am passionate about creating bespoke window treatments and soft furnishings. I had the pleasure of supporting DIY SOS for an edition of their show based locally. I cater to a variety of homes, including barges, B&Bs, and bungalows, offering personalized services that transform spaces with custom curtains, Roman blinds, and a curated selection of high-quality fabrics. Let me help you bring your vision to life with my dedication and expertise.
